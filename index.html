Who we are

We are a community of surfers with a passion for Jesus. We are dedicated to reach every surfing community to share the love of Christ. Passion for surfing, passion for Jesus.

We do this by living out our core values which you can find on the about us page.

What we do

We surf, we skate, we live life yogether. We surf and we serve. Serving as in picking up trash at a local break through to looking after our sport's elite athletes at an international event, we seek to serve the Lord by loving others and being His hands and feet in our global surfing community.

We train and equip people through a bootcamp program called CSALT. which stand for Christian Surfers Acadamy Leadership Training. Besides that we have bible studies once a month. On top of that, every 2 years we celebrate life together on the international conference, were surfers from all over the world come together to have fun and praise Jesus together. Get to know more about this on the get involved page
